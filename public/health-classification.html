About Our Health Classification System

Our advanced health classification model analyzes multiple physiological and environmental parameters to assess your crop's health status. The system evaluates nutrient levels, environmental conditions, and plant responses to provide a comprehensive health assessment.

For accurate results, please provide measurements from reliable sensors and testing methods. The classification can help you identify potential issues early and take corrective actions to maintain optimal crop health.
