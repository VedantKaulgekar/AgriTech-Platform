How Our Disease Detection Works

Our advanced AI model analyzes images of crop leaves to identify signs of disease. The system compares your image against thousands of known disease patterns to provide accurate detection and treatment recommendations.

For best results, take clear photos of affected leaves against a neutral background in good lighting conditions. Focus on areas showing discoloration, spots, or unusual patterns.
