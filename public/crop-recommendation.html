How Our Recommendation System Works

Our crop recommendation system uses machine learning algorithms trained on thousands of data points to analyze your soil and environmental conditions. The system compares your inputs with optimal growing conditions for various crops to determine the best matches.

For accurate recommendations, please provide measurements from soil tests and local weather stations when possible. The more precise your inputs, the better our recommendations will be.
